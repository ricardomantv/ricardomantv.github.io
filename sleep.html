VR Sleep Paralysis

Overview

Roles: Actor, Editor

Tools Used: Ricoh Theta S, Adobe Premiere CC

Collaborators: Apoorva Ramesh, Sijie Ren, Howard Won, Yidi Zhu

This 360/VR horror film was created as the fourth project for Visual Story, Carnegie Mellon Entertainment Technology Center's introductory filmmaking class. This video allows the viewer to experience first-hand sleep paralysis, a condition where you are mentally aware of your surroundings but unable to physically move, and is often paired with unsettling hallucinations.

Intent

For this project, our team wanted to create a narrative that would take advantage of the first-person nature of a VR film, and one that would also use the technical limitation of requiring the camera to be stationary as a strength. We quickly landed on the subject of sleep paralysis since it would hit both of these criteria. The video is based on my own personal experience with sleep paralysis, as well as the local legend of "The Bunnyman" from my hometown.
