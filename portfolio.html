The Portfolio of Ricardo Tucker

Island Exploration

A virtual reality research project to develop a new way for users to explore a virtual space larger than their physical space.

Lawn Darts VR

A simple virtual reality game where players make their last stand against mobs of angry parents trying to ban the game of lawn darts.

Scotty3D Application

A C++ application that features basic 3D modeling operations, raytracing algorithms, material rendering, and animations and physics simulations.

Character Rigs

Two separate character rigs made as part of the Character Rigging for Production course at Carnegie Mellon.

The Attic

A digital playground of two children's attic, where players can interact with objects throughout the scene and try to uncover the mystery of the children that lived there.

Legends of the Hidden Temple: The Booth Game

A game show experience based on the 90's television show, Legends of the Hidden Temple. Built as part of Sigma Phi Epsilon's booth for the CMU Spring 2016 Carnival.
