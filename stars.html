Shota-ing Stars

Overview

Roles: Compositor, Editor

Tools Used: Adobe After Effects CC, Adobe Premiere CC

This video is a fun side project of mine where I turned one of my friends, Shota (hence the name of the video), into a Shooting Stars meme.

Intent

To be entirely honest, there wasn't much thought behind this project besides the novelty of making my own Shooting Stars video, so one slow Saturday morning I just made this to kill time and get back into After Effects.

Process

Shooting Stars videos always feature a person, animal, object, etc. floating around in space to the music while looping the same 1-2 second action repeatedly. So the first step was to isolate a part of the Shota video that I would be able to loop repeatedly. I ultimately decided on his head movement down the collar of his shirt. I

Product

I debuted the video publicly on my personal Facebook page, adding the message "Tag a friend and don't say anything" to the top as another layer to the joke since many Facebook meme/joke videos feature this same message. This actually wound up making the video go semi-viral, since many of my friends actually did tag their friends (even if they didn't personally know Shota), and the video was even shared to several Carnegie Mellon meme pages. As of writing this, the video has just under 6,500 views and 237 Facebook reactions.
